PROJECT CCT

TCross-Country Travels (CCT) is a road transport company operating in West Africa. The company operate luxurious bus travel, mini bus travel and car travel which comprises of individual booking, group booking and private hire. Currently bookings are made in-office and through phone calls. For in-country travel, the only requirement for booking is full payment whilst travellers must provide their international passport for inter-country travel with their full payment as well. The travel company is currently facing some major challenges which include drop in booking, this has been traced back to changes in customer behaviour and financials. They have identified that new customers prefer to book online, and others will like to spread payment to ease the financial hardship of making full payments at once. Also, some customer will like to book their travel in advance to enable them plan properly. CCT are in talks with Digital Echoes to help design and build an Online Booking System. As a result, I have been task to help see this project delivered successfully.
